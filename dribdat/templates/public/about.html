This is an open source project board designed for running splendid hackathons. With this app you can collect and showcase all projects from an event in one place. The name is an amalgam of Driven By Data: we started this platform to help teams sustain their efforts and drive their ideas forward.

If you would like to use this platform for your event, contact the organisers of the events listed here and they should be able to help you - or just fork our open source project and set it up to your liking.
